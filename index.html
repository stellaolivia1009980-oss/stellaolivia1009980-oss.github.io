Our Story

This restaurant began as a simple idea — to create classic food made with care, served in a calm, welcoming space. What started as a shared passion quickly grew into something meaningful, built with love, patience, and a deep respect for food and hospitality.

Menu

Our menu focuses on timeless dishes made fresh in-house. Seasonal ingredients, homemade pasta, and thoughtful presentation are at the heart of everything we serve.

Our Team

Stella is one of the original minds behind the restaurant. She mainly works as a server and is often one of the first faces guests see when they walk in. Stella focuses on making everyone feel comfortable, welcomed, and happy, bringing warmth and personality to every table.

Lon is the co-founder and head chef behind the menu. He created most of the dishes with a focus on classic cooking done right. Nearly all food is made in-house, including homemade pasta, with attention to quality, care, and detail.

Contact
